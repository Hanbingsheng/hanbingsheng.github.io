Hi, I’m Han Bingsheng, as a seasoned designer with a rich background in e-commerce, brand operation, and both mobile app and web design, I have honed a deep understanding of interface design and user experience. My career has seen me delve into the intricacies of vehicle system design, where I’ve developed a keen eye for the nuances of intelligent cockpit design.

My most recent role at Sany Group allowed me to lead the design of vehicle systems for a range of pile driving machines. This included overseeing the creation of digital products such as web-based fleet management systems and mobile digital query systems. During my tenure, I successfully contributed to the development of various vehicle system projects, including general vehicle system solutions compliant with National IV emission standards. This experience has equipped me with a comprehensive skill set in designing user-centric interfaces and optimizing user experiences across diverse platforms.
